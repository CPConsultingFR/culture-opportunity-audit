What Is Company Culture?

Culture isn't about ping-pong tables or company values on the wall. Culture is how your organization actually gets work done - the unwritten rules, shared behaviors, and decision-making patterns that drive (or derail) business results.

Culture shows up in:

  • How decisions get made and who's really involved
  • What behaviors get rewarded (not just what you say you value)
  • How people respond when things go wrong or pressure increases
  • Whether teams collaborate or compete with each other
  • How quickly you adapt to market changes or new opportunities

When culture is aligned with strategy, everything accelerates. When it's misaligned, even the best strategies fail.
